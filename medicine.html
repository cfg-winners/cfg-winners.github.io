General practicians treat all common medical conditions and refer patients with serious conditions to other espetialists. They see patients of all different ages in their own homes and can perform small surgeries like for severe alergic reaction and athma attacks. Learn more about working as a general practitioner here.

Surgeons use operative manual and instrumental techniques on a patient to investigate or treat a pathological condition such as a disease or injury, to help improve bodily function or appearance or to repair unwanted ruptured areas. Training to become a surgeon takes time and is competitive and not everyone who starts the process ends up finishing it. To learn more about the requirements to become a surgeon click here.

Physiotherapy helps to restore movement and function when someone is affected by injury, illness or disability. It can also help to reduce your risk of injury or illness in the future. Phisiotherapists work with patients of all ages with many different conditions to make sure they improve their phisical abilities. Find out more about a career as physiotherapist. here
