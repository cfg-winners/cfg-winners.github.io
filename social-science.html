Therapists work in a number of fields related to the body and mind, and help patients deal with mental, emotional and physical issues, create specific therapies and exercises for the individual, and develop plans for ongoing care to improve their lives. There are several types of therapists, which include physical therapists, occupational therapists, recreational therapists, respiratory therapists, massage therapists, radiation therapists, and mental health, family and marriage therapists. If you're empathetic, patient, understanding and willing to help, this might just be for you. Find out more about this line of work here.

Human resources representatives are arguably the most valuable and unique assets of an organisation as they are responsible for attracting, managing, motivating and developing employees for the benefit of the organisation. They recruit, screen, interview and place workers, but may also handle employee relations, payroll, benefits, and training.
