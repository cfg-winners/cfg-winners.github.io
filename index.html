We're a group of girls, who decided to take on a new challenge and begin a new path into the world of tech, and our first challenge we set ourselves is to build a website which we think will contribute to our society

What we do?

The aim of website is to create a space for people who are heading towards the next phase of life and don't necessarily know what to do with the subjects they enjoy and would like to take futher as a career option.

As a millenial, there are a vast range of career choices, and this website could be a good place to start to view alternative career paths, rather than your generic paths, such as studying medicine to become a GP.

Why?

There's so many career options in this day and age, that our eyes are not opened to, it's time everyone knew what their potential could unlock.
