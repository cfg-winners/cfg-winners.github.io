In chemistry you can have many different careers inside and outside the lab. Physics is one of the most fundamental scientific disciplines, and its main goal is to understand how the universe behaves. Physicists study energy, forces, mechanics, waves, the structure of atoms and the physical universe. Physics has branches like astrophysics, quantum mechanics, optics, cosmology, relativity and many others. Find out about career paths in physics here

Biology is the natural science that studies life and living organisms, including their physical structure, chemical processes, molecular interactions, physiological mechanisms, development and evolution. There are many careers you can follow with a biology degree. Like microbiology, marine biology, pharmacology and many other. Check out more about a career in biology here
